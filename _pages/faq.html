WHAT IS THE ESTIMATED TRAVEL TIME FROM AREA AIRPORTS TO THE FREDERICK?

By car, estimated travel times are as follows:

  • John F. Kennedy International Airport: 50 min.
  • LaGuardia Airport: 30 min.
  • Newark Liberty International Airport: 50 min.

IS THE HOTEL NEAR PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION?

Yes, The Frederick is located near a number of MTA subway stations. Trains within walking distance include: 1/2/3 trains (Chambers St. Station), A/C trains (Chambers St. Station), and the R train (City Hall Station).

DOES THE FREDERICK OFFER FREE WI-FI?

Yes, we are pleased to offer complimentary high-speed wireless internet access for your convenience.

DOES THE FREDERICK OFFER PARKING SERVICE?

Yes, our Tribeca hotel is pleased to offer valet parking service for our guests.

HOW CLOSE IS THE FREDERICK TO MIDTOWN MANHATTAN?

Times Square is only a 10 minute ride away via the 2/3 express train, which stops at the Chambers St. Station on our block.

CAN WE BOOK THEATER AND EVENT TICKETS THROUGH THE HOTEL?

Yes. Please email our hotel at info@triumphny.com

CAN WE STORE BELONGINGS AT THE HOTEL?

Due to limited storage capacity, please contact us ahead of your stay in regard to storing luggage, boxes, bicycles and other equipment - thank you.
