Since 1838

AN EXTRAORDINARY EXAMPLE OF
A HISTORIC HOTEL IN TRIBECA

One of the longest running historic hotels in TriBeCa, the Frederick Hotel was originally built in 1838 as a Gothic Revival structure with an additional building added in 1852, designed in an Italianate style. The Girard House as it was originally named became known as one of the city's finest establishments of its kind.
