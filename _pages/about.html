WHY CHOOSE OR TRIBECA
HOTEL IN LOWER MANHATTAN?

The Frederick Hotel has a storied past and a vibrant future. As timeless as Tribeca, The Frederick remains a desirable destination for visitors to the city that never sleeps. Our hotel in Lower Manhattan puts you in the heart of downtown.

Our historic TriBeCa hotel in NYC is known for providing exceptional service, comfortable accommodations, and luxurious amenities in a classic downtown atmosphere. Each of the rooms at our downtown Manhattan hotel are well-equipped with modern amenities such as complimentary Wi-Fi, iPod docks, and HDTVs and our ideal location in Tribeca is perfect for business and leisure travelers alike.
